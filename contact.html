Common questions

FAQs

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Do you provide demos or trials before purchase?

Yes — for most equipment categories we can arrange demonstrations either at your facility or at our Penang office. Contact us to arrange a time.

Do you provide technical support after purchase?

Absolutely. After-sales support is a core part of what we do. Our team provides installation, commissioning, training and ongoing technical assistance.

Can you supply to Singapore or Thailand?

Yes. We serve manufacturers in Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand and China. Contact us with your location and requirements and we will advise on logistics.

How long does delivery typically take?

Delivery times vary by product and origin. Standard items from stock ship within 1–2 weeks. Imported equipment from principals typically takes 4–8 weeks. We will give you a clear timeline at the time of quotation.

What brands do you carry?

Our main principals include Phoseon Technology, Vermes Microdispensing, EIT Instruments and San Ei Tech. We also supply a range of materials and consumables under the Eprocent brand.

I'm not sure which product I need — can you help?

Yes, this is one of the most common enquiries we receive. Describe your application or process in the contact form and our engineers will recommend the most suitable solution.
