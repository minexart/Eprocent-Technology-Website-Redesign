UV curing
Phoseon Technology

Phoseon Technology pioneered the use of LEDs for curing in both commercial and industrial applications, offering equipment manufacturers high-performance curing technology that provides maximum UV energy, superior performance and high reliability for demanding applications.

Phoseon products primarily utilise 395nm wavelength, but can also be built with other wavelength diodes. Available in air-cooled and water-cooled configurations.

UV measurement
EIT Instruments

EIT is the industry standard for UV measurement instruments, supplying radiometers and profilers used globally for process validation, compliance documentation and quality control in UV curing applications.

EIT instruments measure peak irradiance (W/cm²) and energy density (J/cm²) across UV bandwidths — essential for establishing correct UV levels and maintaining consistent cure quality.

Why switch to UV LED?
