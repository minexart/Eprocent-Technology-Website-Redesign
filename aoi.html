World-leading AOI with Dynamic Process Control

Machine Vision Products is the world's leading supplier of measurement-based Automated Optical Inspection solutions for SMT, SPI, microelectronics and packaging companies. Their award-winning Dynamic Process Control (DPC) solutions provide leading process control capabilities that go beyond simple pass/fail inspection.

DPC enables real-time process feedback — using inspection data to actively control upstream processes and prevent defects before they occur, rather than simply detecting them after the fact. This closes the loop between inspection and production, delivering measurable gains in first-pass yield, asset utilisation and overall efficiency.

Eprocent Technology supplies and supports Machine Vision Products AOI systems in Malaysia, Singapore and Thailand.
