Jet dispensing
Vermes Microdispensing

Vermes is an independent producer of low-maintenance piezo MicroDispensing Systems offering maximum accuracy and dependability. The MDS product family uses high-power piezo stacks for extremely fast valve opening and closing — up to 1,000Hz at full stroke.

Applications include micro gluing, micro marking, micro lubricating, micro spraying and fine dispensing of almost every customer-specific media. The modular design means users can switch between jetting and spray applications by changing only the nozzle and fluidbox — no new valve body required.

Auto dispensing
San Ei Tech

San Ei Tech supplies fully automatic and semi-automatic dispensing systems for electronics manufacturing. Their auto dispensers offer programmable XYZ motion control for consistent adhesive, epoxy and solder paste application at production speed.

From benchtop semi-auto units for prototyping and rework, to fully automated inline systems for volume production — San Ei Tech covers the full range of dispensing automation requirements.
