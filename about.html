Built on trust,
driven by engineering

Since 2002, Eprocent Technology has been supplying precision engineering solutions to Malaysia's most demanding manufacturers.

More than two decades of engineering expertise

Eprocent Technology Sdn Bhd was established in Penang, Malaysia — the heart of Southeast Asia's electronics manufacturing corridor. From day one, our mission has been straightforward: provide manufacturers with the right tools, backed by people who actually understand the processes.

We don't just sell equipment. We understand how dispensing, curing, inspection and soldering systems are used in real production environments, and we stand behind every product we supply with genuine technical support.

Over two decades, we've built long-term relationships with manufacturers across Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand and China — supplying equipment from world-class principals including Phoseon, Vermes and EIT Instruments.

Our journey

How we grew

Two decades of building relationships, expanding our range, and deepening our technical expertise.

2002
Founded
Eprocent Technology established in Penang

Started as a specialist supplier of dispensing equipment and process materials for Penang's growing electronics manufacturing sector.

2006
Expansion
UV curing systems added to portfolio

Partnered with Phoseon Technology and EIT Instruments to bring UV LED curing and measurement solutions to Malaysian manufacturers.

2011
Regional growth
Expanded into Singapore and Thailand

Growing demand from regional manufacturers led us to extend our sales and support coverage across Southeast Asia.

2016
Portfolio
AOI and robotic systems introduced

Added automated optical inspection and robotic automation to meet the growing demand for quality control and process automation.

Now
Today
Full-range engineering solutions provider

Serving manufacturers across MY, SG, TH and CN with 10+ product lines and a team of engineers who understand how every product is used in the field.
