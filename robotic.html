Robotic automation
systems

Tabletop XYZ robots, SCARA systems and integrated dispensing cells for consistent, repeatable electronics manufacturing automation.

Automate your dispensing and assembly for consistent results at scale

Robotic automation removes the natural variation that comes with manual processes — operator fatigue, positioning differences and speed inconsistency. Once programmed, a robot delivers the same result every cycle, every shift, at production speed.

Our robotic systems are a natural extension of our dispensing product line. A Vermes MDS jet dispenser or San Ei Tech dispensing valve mounted on a 3-axis or SCARA robot gives you precision fluid application with fully automated XYZ positioning — combining the best of both product ranges into a single integrated solution.

Every system is commissioned and supported by our Penang engineering team. We help you integrate it into your process, not just deliver the hardware.
